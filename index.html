About me

Welcome to my personal website! My name is Nayir and I am an aspiring web developer who began coding as a self-taught learner. With a good amount of perseverance, curiosity, and countless hours spent learning programming languages, I managed to acquire a good understanding of Node.js, React, TypeScript, PHP and many other technologies. My passion for web development has culminated in three successful projects, each challenging me in unique ways and fueling my desire to learn and grow. As a beginner developer, I am ready to start my career and apply these skills in a real-world environment. I am highly motivated to secure my first experience in the web development industry, and contribute to a team with my creative problem-solving skills, curiosity, and dedication.
